# Customer Management - User Guide

**Module:** Customer Management  
**Application:** Web Portal  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Date:** 2026-03-24

---

## Overview

The Customer Management module allows you to create, view, and manage customer records in the banking system. You can handle three types of customers: Corporate, SME, and Individual.

---

## Accessing Customer Management

1. Log in to the banking portal
2. Click on **Customers** in the main navigation menu
3. The Customer List page will be displayed

---

## Customer List View

The Customer List page shows all customers with:
- **Customer Number** - Unique identifier
- **Name** - Customer name (registered name or individual name)
- **Type** - CORPORATE, SME, or INDIVIDUAL
- **Status** - ACTIVE, PENDING, RESTRICTED, or CLOSED
- **Created Date** - When the customer was added

### Searching Customers

Use the search bar to find specific customers:
- Type a name, customer number, or email
- Results update as you type

### Filtering

Filter customers by:
- **Type** - Corporate, SME, or Individual
- **Status** - Active, Pending, Restricted, Closed

---

## Creating a New Customer

### Starting a New Customer

1. From the Customer List, click the **Add Customer** or **+ New Customer** button
2. Select the customer type:
   - **Corporate** - For large businesses
   - **SME** - For small/medium enterprises
   - **Individual** - For personal customers

### Creating a Corporate Customer

Fill in the corporate details:

**Business Information:**
- **Registered Name** - Official business name
- **Trading Name** - Common name (if different)
- **Registration Number** - Business registration number
- **Industry** - Select from the dropdown
- **Annual Revenue** - Business turnover
- **Currency** - Primary currency

**Address:**
- **Street Address** - Physical address
- **City** - City
- **Country** - Select country
- **Postal Code** - ZIP/Postal code

**Contact Person:**
- **Name** - Contact person's name
- **Email** - Contact email
- **Phone** - Contact phone number

Click **Save** or **Create** to add the customer.

### Creating an SME Customer

Fill in the SME details:

**Business Information:**
- **Business Name** - Company name
- **Registration Number** - Business registration
- **Business Type** - Sole proprietorship, Limited company, etc.
- **Industry** - Business sector
- **Annual Turnover** - Yearly revenue
- **Currency** - Primary currency

**Address:**
- Fill in the business address

**Contact Details:**
- Contact name, email, and phone

Click **Save** to create the SME customer.

### Creating an Individual Customer

Fill in personal details:

**Personal Information:**
- **First Name** - Given name
- **Last Name** - Family name
- **Date of Birth** - Select date
- **Gender** - Select from dropdown
- **National ID** - ID card/passport number
- **Nationality** - Country of citizenship

**Address:**
- Home address

**Contact:**
- **Email** - Email address
- **Phone** - Phone number

Click **Save** to create the individual customer.

---

## Viewing Customer Details

1. From the Customer List, click on a customer's row
2. The Customer Detail page shows:
   - Basic information
   - Address details
   - Contact information
   - Account summary (if any)
   - Activity history

---

## Editing a Customer

1. From the Customer Detail page, click **Edit** or **Edit Customer**
2. Modify the necessary fields
3. Click **Save** to update

**Note:** Some fields may be read-only depending on customer status.

---

## Customer Status

### Understanding Statuses

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **PENDING** | New customer awaiting approval |
| **ACTIVE** | Fully operational customer |
| **RESTRICTED** | Limited operations (e.g., KYC pending) |
| **CLOSED** | Customer account closed |

### Changing Status

Contact your administrator or supervisor to change customer status.

## KYC (Know Your Customer)

### What is KYC?

KYC is a regulatory requirement to verify customer identity.

### Viewing KYC Status

1. Open a customer record
2. Look for the **KYC** section
3. Shows verification status and documents

### Submitting KYC Documents

1. In the customer record, go to **KYC Documents**
2. Click **Add Document** or **Upload**
3. Select document type:
   - Passport
   - National ID
   - Driver's License
   - Utility Bill
4. Enter document details
5. Upload the document
6. Click **Save**

---

## Authorizations

For business customers (Corporate/SME), you can manage authorized signatories.

### Viewing Authorizations

1. Open a customer record
2. Navigate to **Authorizations** or **Signatories**
3. Shows all authorized persons

### Adding an Authorized Person

1. In Authorizations, click **Add Authorization**
2. Fill in:
   - **Name** - Full name
   - **Authorization Type** - Signatory, Delegate, etc.
   - **Access Level** - Full or Limited
   - **Valid From** - Start date
   - **Valid To** - End date
3. Click **Save**

## Best Practices

1. **Complete Information**
   - Fill in all required fields
   - Verify information before saving

2. **KYC Compliance**
   - Submit KYC documents promptly
   - Keep documents up to date

3. **Authorization Management**
   - Maintain current authorized signatories
   - Set appropriate expiry dates
   - Remove expired authorizations

## Troubleshooting

### Can't Create Customer

- **Missing required fields**: Fill in all required information
- **Duplicate**: Check if customer already exists
- **Permission denied**: Contact administrator

### Can't Edit Customer

- **Read-only**: Some fields cannot be edited after creation
- **Status restrictions**: Some fields locked for certain statuses

### Can't View Customer

- **Permission denied**: Request access from administrator
